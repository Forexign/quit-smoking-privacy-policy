Stop Smoking: Quit Tracker

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set my quit date?
When you first open the app, you'll be guided through the onboarding where you can set your quit date, daily cigarette count, and pack price. You can update these anytime from the Settings screen.

How is my money saved calculated?
Money saved is calculated based on the number of cigarettes you would have smoked since your quit date, multiplied by the price per cigarette you entered during setup.

How do I unlock achievements?
Achievements are unlocked automatically as you reach time and health milestones. Keep your quit streak going and they'll appear with a celebration animation.

Can I reset my progress?
Yes. Go to Settings → Reset Data. Note that this will permanently delete your progress.

Is my data stored in the cloud?
All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload personal data to any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.

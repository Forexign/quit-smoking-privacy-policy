# Privacy Policy - Sigaradan Kurtul

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Şükrü Taşkıran (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use the Sigaradan Kurtul - Quit Smoking mobile application (“App”).

Sigaradan Kurtul is a smoking cessation and habit tracking app. The App is designed to store your personal quit-smoking progress primarily on your own device. We do not operate user accounts, cloud sync, or a backend database for your personal smoking journal or progress data.

## 1. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App may ask you to enter information such as:

- Quit date
- Previous cigarette usage
- Cigarette pack price and quantity
- Progress, savings, milestones, achievements, and app preferences
- Journal entries, notes, motivations, or relapse-related information you choose to enter
- A motivation video selected from your device, if you choose to use that feature

This information is stored locally on your device. We do not receive, view, sell, or share this local quit-smoking progress data.

If you delete the App, the locally stored data may be deleted from your device, subject to your operating system’s backup and restore behavior.

## 2. Information Collected Through Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services to support advertising, purchases, product analytics, and app functionality. These services may process certain data according to their own privacy policies.

### Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to show advertisements to non-paying users.

Google AdMob may collect or process information such as:

- Advertising identifiers, where available and permitted
- Device information
- IP address
- Approximate location inferred from IP address
- Ad interactions
- Diagnostic and performance information
- Consent signals and ad personalization preferences

Advertising may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your region, consent status, device settings, and applicable law.

For users in regions where consent is required, the App uses Google’s User Messaging Platform to request and manage advertising consent. Where required, you may manage your advertising privacy choices from the App’s settings.

More information:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

### RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscription/supporter status.

RevenueCat may process information such as:

- App user identifier generated for purchase management
- Purchase history and entitlement status
- Product identifiers
- Store transaction information received from Apple App Store or Google Play

We do not receive your full payment card details. Payments are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play.

More information:
- RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### Mixpanel

We use Mixpanel for product analytics to understand how users interact with the App and to improve the product.

Mixpanel may process information such as:

- App events, for example screen views, onboarding steps, feature usage, settings interactions, and purchase-related status
- Device type, operating system, app version, language, and region
- Approximate location inferred from IP address
- A pseudonymous app/user identifier

We do not intentionally send your smoking journal text, motivation notes, exact quit-smoking history, or other sensitive quit progress details to Mixpanel.

Mixpanel is used for product analytics, not for selling personal data or advertising brokerage.

More information:
- Mixpanel Privacy Policy: https://mixpanel.com/legal/privacy-policy/

### Apple App Store and Google Play

If you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. Their privacy policies apply to payment processing and store account activity.

More information:
- Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 3. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

We do not intentionally collect:

- Your name
- Email address, unless you contact us directly
- Phone number
- Contacts
- Precise GPS location
- Photos or videos, except local access to a video you choose for in-app use
- Health records from Apple Health, Google Health Connect, or medical providers
- Smoking journal text or personal motivation notes on our servers

## 4. How We Use Information

Information may be used to:

- Provide core App functionality
- Store your progress locally on your device
- Show advertisements to non-paying users
- Manage consent and privacy choices
- Process purchases and supporter access
- Understand aggregate product usage
- Improve App stability, design, and features
- Respond to support requests if you contact us

## 5. Advertising and Consent Choices

The App may show ads through Google AdMob.

Depending on your region, you may be asked to consent to personalized advertising and related data processing. You may also be able to manage advertising choices through:

- The consent message shown in the App
- The App’s settings, where available
- Your device advertising settings
- Platform privacy controls provided by Apple or Google

If you decline advertising consent where applicable, the App may still show non-personalized ads.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your local quit-smoking progress data.

We may share limited information with third-party service providers only as needed to operate the App, including:

- Google AdMob for advertising
- RevenueCat for purchase management
- Mixpanel for product analytics
- Apple App Store and Google Play for purchase processing

These providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

## 7. Data Retention

Local App data remains on your device until you delete it, reset the App data, or uninstall the App.

Third-party providers may retain data according to their own retention policies. You may contact us if you want assistance with privacy or deletion requests related to data we can reasonably control.
